## [0.0.1]

Initial release.

Supported string types are:

* `names::Name`,
* `names::Ncname`,
* `names::Qname`, `names::ParsedQname`,
* `names::UriQualifiedName`, `names::ParsedUriQualifiedName`, and
* `names::Nmtoken`.
